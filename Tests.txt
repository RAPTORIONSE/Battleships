-Setup Snapping
When droping selected pieces do they snap into the grid?

In the setup phase.
1. Hover mouse over a ship.
2. Click and hold left mouse button.
3. Drag mouse over to the grid and align the piece with the grid.
4. Release left mouse button.

The piece should snap to the grid you aligned it to.

-Setup Snapping directions
When droping selected pieces do they snap into the grid?

In the setup phase.
1. Hover mouse over a ship.
2. Click and hold left mouse button.
3. Drag mouse over to the grid and align the piece with the grid.
4. Drag mouse slightly less then half a grid in any direction.
5. Release left mouse button.

The piece should snap to the grid you aligned it to.

-Setup Snapping directions rotated
When droping selected pieces that are rotated do they snap into the grid?

In the setup phase.
1. Hover mouse over a ship.
2. Click and hold left mouse button.
3. Press 'R' on the keyboard.
3. Drag mouse over to the grid and align the piece with the grid.
4. Drag mouse slightly less then half a grid in any direction.
5. Release left mouse button.

The piece should snap to the grid you aligned it to and remain rotated by 90 degrees.

All ships should be placed on the grid without overlapping.

-Fire aiming
Does the fire grid highlight the grid bellow the mouse?

In fire phase.
1. Hover mouse over any grid in the fire grid.

The grid should display a lighter colour then its neighbours.

-fire shooting
When clicking a grid in the fire grid does the grid show an impact marker, and does it play an sfx corresponing to the marker?

In fire phase.
1. Hover mouse over any grid in the fire grid.
2. Click left mouse button.

Game should show a hitmarker either a circle or cross. A firing sound should play immediately and after a short delay a splash or impact sound should play.

-fire sinking
When firing at a ship and all its compartments/grids are hit does it revleal itself to the otherplayers fire grid, and does it play a sinking sound? 

In fire phase.
1. Fire at grids until a ship is found, indicated by a hitmarker (cross).
2. Fire at the neighbouring grids to identify what direction the ship is facing.
3. Fire at the neighbouring grids until the ship is surrounded by missmarkers (circles)

Did the ship appear when all its grids had been hit, and did it play a sinking sound?

-fire winning
When the last hostile ship sinks does the game reveal the correct winner?

In fire phase.
1. Fire at the hostile fleet unitl all ships are sunk.
2. Restart game with the button.
3. As the opposite player, Fire at the hostile fleet unitl all ships are sunk.

The game should revieal a victory screen when all ships are sunk, the score should increment for the player that survived. The score at the end of the test shoudl be 1 / 1.
